That's my Journey

Initially, I started as a technical support in a company where I stayed for more than 7 years, which allowed me to develop skills such as communication, adaptability, teamwork, critical thinking, among others. However, after so much time providing support and dealing with users, I wanted more. My creativity couldn't be limited, I wanted to solve problems and not just explain and train people.

I sought an opportunity as a developer in the market and it emerged at Imply, where I have been for 2 years, and where I took my first steps as a developer. I worked in a team on high-performance and scalable projects, using agile methodologies such as Kanban.

As expected, I am now pursuing more and aiming to become a full-stack developer, refining my skills in design and UX.
